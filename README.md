# SickB Chain: The Dawn of Post-Ethereum Blockchain Evolution

**Technical Whitepaper v2.0 · March 2026**

325 pages. 22 chapters. Technical, literary, didactic. A hybrid between engineering documentation and storytelling. Based on real events, real code, real architecture.

## About

This whitepaper documents the complete technical architecture, economics, governance, and human story behind SickB Chain, a custom Arbitrum Orbit Layer 3 blockchain built by a solo developer, funded entirely from his own capital, with no venture capital backing.

It was written for three audiences simultaneously. Someone discovering blockchain for the first time. A developer evaluating the architecture for technical merit. An investor who needs to understand what separates this from the thousands of projects that came before.

Every concept is introduced through analogy first, then explained with full technical precision. Progressive complexity that builds chapter by chapter.
